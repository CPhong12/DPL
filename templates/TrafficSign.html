About our Project

Traffic Sign Recognition (TSR) is at the core of our project, and our focus is on enhancing road safety and efficiency. Using advanced computer vision and deep learning technologies, our project enables the robust identification and classification of traffic signs in images and static frames. Whether it's detecting speed limits, stop signs, or directional indicators, our system excels in providing accurate information and enhancing driver awareness. We are dedicated to promoting safer road conditions and more effective traffic management through the power of artificial intelligence. Our mission is to contribute to the improvement of road safety by harnessing cutting-edge technology, all without the need for real-time video. Join us in our commitment to making our roads safer for all.


Why Traffic sign recognization

Reduction in human error:

Human drivers may miss or misinterpret traffic signs due to various factors like distractions, fatigue, or poor visibility. TSR systems can accurately and consistently detect and interpret traffic signs, reducing the chances of human error and improving compliance with traffic regulations.

Enhancing road safety:

By alerting drivers to important traffic sign information, TSR helps prevent accidents, especially those caused by speeding, wrong turns, or failure to yield. It promotes safer driving behavior and adherence to traffic rules, contributing to overall road safety.
